MESSAGE FROM THE FOUNDER

Dear students, parents, staff of KA Academy and our visitors,

Welcome to KETI Academy learning community. We are delighted that you visited our site and exploring what we do. We welcome your feedback on how to make our school better.

We are living in a time where most of us are asking ourselves, “What can I do to contribute positively to the educational crisis that our Nation is facing today?” I believe one of the solutions to improving our educational performance is by investing in preschool and primary level education; through establishing quality learning environment, qualified and well paid staff, and the overall resourcing of schools.

As one who have been brought up in this country, I too felt that I cannot stay on the sideline as our brilliant young minds are calling us for help. That is why we established KETI Academy to complement, and not to compete with local schools.

We have set standards on most aspects of teaching and learning activities at KA. We invite you to come and visit us, volunteer if you can, send our dedicated teachers and support staff encouraging messages. We seek your wisdom and understanding.

Together we can make a difference!
Michael Alemu, Founder.
