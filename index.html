Our Service

Potential at KETI Academy

KETI Academy is committed to help our students discover and grow to reach their God given potential in their education and personal development .

Located in Wolkessa, Assela, we provide Nursery to KG at KETI 1 Academy, and Grade 1 to 8, at KETI 2 Primary School site.(GOOGLE MAP)

KA Snack and Lunch Program

    KETI has exceptional experienced team of cooks who prepare nutritious, delicious and health meals and snacks enjoyed by all our Nursery and KG students at KETI 1. It is an absolute joy to see the children enjoy their mealtime with social activities, sharing with manners and equality at their own cafeteria.

KA School Transport Service

For children who live in Assela, we provide school bus services with adult supervision to and from school. The morning and afternoon services stop at convenient locations on the Main Assela Road from Dinsho Condominium to Bale Berr routes.
