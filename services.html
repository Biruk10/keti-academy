Our Services

Nursery and KG: Nursery and KG School provides child centered care and kindergarten education to prepare children for primary school education. The children are provided with play-based learning, while they develop physically, emotionally, and basic academic learning in both Oromiffa, and the Amharic languages.

Primary School: Consistent with the Ethiopian Curriculum and best practices, KETI Primary (Elementary) School provides high level academic and technical programs in small class settings, helping our students to establish a solid foundation for higher education.

Students learning goals are developed consistent with National educational goals, and guidelines as well as adopting international standards for learning. For example, target for grades, visibility of students in class, gaining hands-on experience, managing time, productivity to complete homework, diligence om quality of work, physical fitness and sports, competitiveness, creativity, health, interpersonal skills, teamwork, leadership skills, public speaking, environmental awareness, etc.

Food Service

    KETI has exceptional experienced team of cooks who prepare nutritious, delicious and health meals and snacks enjoyed by all our Nursery and KG students at KETI 1. It is an absolute joy to see the children enjoy their mealtime with social activities, sharing with manners and equality at their own cafeteria.

KA School Transport Service

For children who live in Assela, we provide school bus services with adult supervision to and from school. The morning and afternoon services stop at convenient locations on the Main Assela Road from Dinsho Condominium to Bale Berr routes.
