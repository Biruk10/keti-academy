INSTRUCTIONAL PHILOSOPHY AND FOCUS

Class Size: The primary focus of education is to help the child discover his or her cognitive, affective, and psychomotor skills. Our class sizes do not exceed 25 students to allow for individualized attention and better student-teacher interaction.

Engaged: We place values on a learning process through participation, contribution and production. Based on MOE guideline, we engage learners through observing, doing and dialogue.

Well-balanced: Our students will be well-balanced who are responsible, accountable, productive members, who will retain their cultural diversity, celebrate their identity, and be able to adopt to a changing world.

Class Size: The primary focus of education is to help the child discover his or her cognitive, affective, and psychomotor skills. Our class sizes do not exceed 25 students to allow for individualized attention and better student-teacher interaction.

Well-balanced: Our students will be well-balanced who are responsible, accountable, productive members, who will retain their cultural diversity, celebrate their identity, and be able to adopt to a changing world.

Love: Love is the foundation of everything we do at KETI Academy. Our outdoor and indoor learning lab create a love for nature and science. Our approach to play-based and interest-driven learning fosters a love of learning. Ongoing attention to the value and dignity of every child results in a love for others.

On-Ramp: Excellent early childhood and elementary education is the strongest predictor of future academic success. KETI Academy will create “on-ramp” to the highway of education, by equipping students with the tools to think critically and ethically. We emphasize Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Maths (STEAM) learning in all we do.

High Impact: Real learning often occurs outside of textbooks and the classroom. Students will be exposed to cultural and educational enrichment beyond our school facility.

High Expectations: Students rise to the learning culture in which they are placed. Every child can succeed and excel. Our goal is for each child to perform on grade level and beyond, rising to the full measure of their God-given ability.

Whole Person: We do not see children as students. We see them as human beings made in the image of a loving God. KETI will nurture each child holistically – spirit, mind, body, and soul.

Well-balanced: Our students will be well-balanced who are responsible, accountable, productive members, who will retain their cultural diversity, celebrate their identity, and be able to adopt to a changing world.

Heritage: KETI learning will encourage children to value their national and international heritage, unity within the diversity of Ethiopia, respect and equality for all people, care for their environment, and to excel in their God given potential.

Staff: Nurturing, creative, emotionally healthy, and financially well compensated. Stringent criminal background checks are done for every staff working at the school.

Facility: A safe, natural, beautiful place. Children in Assela have challenges such as lack of technology, play area, and libraries. Keti Academy will be designed for their needs.

KETI Academy is committed to help our students discover and grow to reach their God given potential in their education and personal development .
